Dependability Modeling with Fault Trees and RBDs

If you need a quick way for drawing and analyzing system availability characteristics, this one is for you. We already offer a free basic account so that you can just start.

Modeling with uncertainties - Introducing FuzzTrees

Our novel concept for fault tree modeling allows you to express configurable and uncertain parts of the system. We are working on an analysis engine that will tell you which configuration options give the best cost-dependability ratio.

Graph sharing for teaching and collaboration

Graphs are planned to be easily sharable between users to support academic teaching and professional collaboration projects.
