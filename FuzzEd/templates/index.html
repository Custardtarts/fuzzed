Fault Trees and RBDs

If you need a quick way for drawing and analyzing system dependability models, this tool is the fastest path to go. We do (and always will) provide a free basic account for non-commercial use.

Make Uncertainties Explicit.

Introducing FuzzTrees

Our novel extended concept for fault tree modeling allows you to express configurable and uncertain parts of the system. Our analysis engine tells you which configuration options of the live system will give the best cost-dependability ratio.
